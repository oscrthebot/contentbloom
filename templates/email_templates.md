## Variables to Customize

For each email, replace:
- `{{store_name}}` - e.g., "Blue Moon Candles"
- `{{first_name}}` - e.g., "Sarah"
- `{{specific_compliment}}` - e.g., "your minimalist product photography" or "your unique candle scents"
- `{{sender_name}}` - Your name
- `{{sender_email}}` - Your contact email

---

## Sending Strategy (Instantly.ai)

1. **Day 0:** Send Template 1 (Intro)
2. **Day 3:** If no reply → Template 2 (Follow-up 1)
3. **Day 5:** If no reply → Template 3 (Follow-up 2)
4. **Day 6+:** If reply = interested → Template 4 (Closing)

**Stop sending if:**
- They reply (any response—positive or negative)
- They click unsubscribe
- Email bounces

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

Try variations:
- Subject lines (emoji vs no emoji)
- Value prop (free vs "portfolio building")
- Length (shorter vs detailed)
- CTA ("Reply YES" vs "Click here" vs "Let's chat")

Track open rates, reply rates, and conversion to actual clients.
